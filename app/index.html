About me:

Hello, my name is Yaser Tallo.
I'm a freelance web developer and a little bit of a designer, currently based in Damascus, Syria.


Detail-oriented, self-motivated, someone who is alway learning and trying out new things just for fun.

I do clean fully responsive websites that looks and works great on any device type or size depending on the user's device and screen, with support for all major browsers and versions as needed.

Clean modular code, following the best practices and trying to stay up-to-date with all modern techniques and technologies. Always keeping performance, speed and maintainability in mind.

Custom websites and CMS's themes and plugins, such as WordPress.

Can work on any project size, wether it is a small scale project, or a larger scale one. A one man's job or a collaboration with a group of people.

Everything is tailored as the project needs, no bloating with un-necessary code or un-used functionalities due to use of frameworks or plugins.
